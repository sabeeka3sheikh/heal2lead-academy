Personalised, root-cause coaching for the woman who is done managing and ready to actually heal.

Before we begin

This is not for everyone.

This container is not a quick fix. It is not a formula you follow or a course you complete. It is deep, personalised work that requires you to be ready — not perfect, not certain, but genuinely willing.

I work with a small number of women at a time. Every application is reviewed personally. This ensures that when we begin, we can go all the way in.

This is for you if:

  • You have tried self-help approaches and are still stuck — you need a personalised guide, not more content
  • You are exhausted from being the strong one and genuinely ready to let that pattern go
  • You want to understand the root cause of what keeps you stuck, not just manage the symptoms
  • You are ready to invest — in time, presence, and energy — in yourself
  • You want work that honours both your psychology and your spiritual foundation

This is not for you if:

  • You are looking for validation rather than transformation
  • You want a step-by-step course or group programme
  • You are not ready to look honestly at your own patterns
  • You are in acute mental health crisis — please seek professional clinical support first

A note on readiness

You don't need to have it together to begin. You don't need to know exactly what the problem is. What you need is a genuine desire to find out — and the willingness to do something different than what hasn't been working.

The Offering

The 1:1 Coaching Container

Built around the H.E.A.L. Framework — personalised to you, your history, and what is actually driving your patterns.

What's included

  • Deep-dive intake session to map your root patterns and history
  • Personalised H.E.A.L. journey built around your specific profile
  • Regular 1:1 sessions with Sabeeka — structured, precise, and held
  • Between-session voice/written support for integration
  • Enneagram and identity mapping relevant to your patterns
  • Tools and practices calibrated to where you actually are

What we address

  • The root belief driving your people-pleasing or self-erasure pattern
  • The identity you took on that no longer serves you
  • The emotional exhaustion that keeps returning no matter what you do
  • Your relationship with boundaries, needs, and self-worth
  • The version of you that exists beneath the performance

Note: This coaching container is for personal growth and emotional empowerment. It is not a substitute for licensed mental health treatment, therapy, or medical care.

Before you apply

Frequently asked questions

How is this different from therapy? +

Coaching is future-focused and action-oriented. It works with where you are and where you want to be. Therapy is a licensed clinical practice focused on mental health diagnosis and treatment. This coaching container is not therapy and is not a substitute for it. If you are managing a clinical mental health condition, I encourage you to work with a licensed therapist.

Do you work with women outside Saudi Arabia? +

Yes. All sessions are conducted online. I work with high-achieving women globally — particularly those in the South Asian and Arab diaspora communities — wherever they are.

How long does the container last? +

Duration is discussed during the clarity call and depends on where you are and what you need. Real transformation is not a 4-week sprint. We design what will actually serve you — not what is most convenient to package.

What is the investment? +

Investment details are shared during the clarity call after we've both determined fit. I don't publish a price because the container is personalised — what we build together depends on what you need. Payment plans are available.
