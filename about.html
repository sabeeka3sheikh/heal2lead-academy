About

The Identity Architect

I help high-achieving women un-become who they were taught to be — so they can become who they were always meant to be.

My Story

I was the strong one.
Until I wasn't.

For years, I was the one who handled it all. The crises. The emotions. The weight of other people's worlds. I managed everything with grace, with composure — and with a quiet exhaustion that no one could see.

I was the daughter who never complained. The friend who always showed up. The professional who never missed a deadline. The woman who held everyone else together while quietly falling apart inside.

I did all the inner work. Journaling. Podcasts. Online courses. Workshops with renowned coaches. I've invested over $60,000 in my own healing. And yet — something was still stuck.

That's when I realized: most coaching addresses behavior. It teaches you to manage symptoms. But it doesn't go deep enough. It doesn't work at the level of identity — the root belief system that keeps you trapped in the same patterns.

So I created the H.E.A.L. Framework™ — my proprietary system for identity-level transformation. Not just feeling better. But becoming different.

The Methodology

The H.E.A.L. Framework™

A 4-pillar approach to healing at the root level.

H

Honor

We begin by honoring where you are — not bypassing it. Your exhaustion is information. Your emotional history is not a wound to hide; it is the map.

E

Excavate

We go beneath the surface to find the root cause — the original belief, wound, or pattern driving your current reality. This is where real change begins.

A

Align

Once the root is clear, we realign your identity — how you see yourself, what you believe you deserve, and how you show up in relationships and work.

L

Lead

From this healed place, you lead — your life, your relationships, your work — with calm authority rather than from fear, guilt, or obligation.
